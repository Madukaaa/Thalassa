Our Mission

To preserve marine ecosystems through sustainable practices for continuous development.

Our mission aligns with UN Sustainable Development Goal 14: Life Below Water. We are responsible for conserving the resources of the marine ecosystem for the advancement and development of mankind through sustainable practices.
